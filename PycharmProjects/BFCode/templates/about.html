  About us  

The vast majority of day-to-day care in type 1 diabetes (T1D) is handled by patients and their families, making appropriate self-management a cornerstone in any successful treatment. Unfortunately, self-management can be a time-consuming and challenging task even for experienced patients, since most of treatment decisions are based on multiple uncertain factors. Therefore, our goal is to provide patients with a powerful hands-on Web Information Tool (WIT) that will empower them to fully leverage the data they generate. Through this novel educational platform, patients are able to easily and quickly assess the impact of self-management practices on their glucose control as well as explore alternatives, leading to better diabetes literacy and glucose control. We aim to offer support through the Adaptive Biobehavioral Control (ABC) application that will tell patients about changes in their insulin needs and recommendations for care, with the possibility of running simulations for the recommendations.
